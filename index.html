# Earthquakes in the displaying region

The purpose of this smartdashboard is visualize the number of earthquakes during January 2024. In bar graph that is visualized above, it's purpose is to illustrate the number of earthquakes in a certain region depending on where the map is zoomed in and its magnitude level. For example, when hovering over the state of California, the bar graph will change to show the total number of earthquakes that have occurred, in addition to their magnitude levels on the x axis. The y axis lists the number of earthquakes. In addition, the proportional symbols are click-able, in which they will list the magnitude of the earthquake that occured in that specific area.
